Our Story

The idea for the shop came about when owner Salim happened across the beautiful Belle Epoche coffee machine one a trip to Rome. It was love at first site and he knew others would be drawn to the machine as he was. Placing it front and center in a shop in Aldgate he used it as the inspiration to create a space of great coffee and great converstation.
